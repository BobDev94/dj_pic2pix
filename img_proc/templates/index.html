About Pic2Pix

Pic2Pix, by BobDev is a Python3 image processing script specifically written to convert images and pictures into sprites usable in 2d game engines such as GameMaker.

To see how the script works, just hit the process button to see how the placeholder image is processed.

To process your own image, hit the Choose File button on the left, and select your image.

Once you have uploaded your image, it will be processed, which can take upto 10 seconds.

In the event that your image is not as good as you expected, or areas of the 'sprite' has been filtered out, this may be because there is little variation in the color of the target and the background. There's some things you can to do ensure a better picture quality.

-Reduce TOLERANCE

-Retake the picture with a more distinctly colored background
